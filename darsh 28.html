About the festival

The Spring Festival 2025 brings together music, dance, food, and art to celebrate the vibrant spirit of our community. Enjoy performances, exhibitions, and delicious cuisine in a joyful atmosphere across three immersive days.

All events are family-friendly and open to the public.

Schedule

Day 1

Music Concert & Dance Performances — Opening night featuring local bands, classical recitals, and contemporary dance.

Day 2

Food Fair & Cultural Programs — Street food, artisan stalls, and folk theatre celebrating regional traditions.

Day 3

Art Exhibition & Closing Ceremony — Visual arts showcase, live painting, and a heartwarming finale.
