Importance of mental health

Importance of Mental Health Good mental health allows people to cope with the normal stresses of life, work productively, and contribute to their community. Poor mental health, if unaddressed, can lead to challenges such as anxiety, depression, substance abuse, and more severe disorders. Common Mental Health Conditions Anxiety Disorders: Characterized by excessive fear or worry. Depression: Persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, or loss of interest. Bipolar Disorder: Marked by extreme mood swings. Schizophrenia and Psychosis: Severe disruptions in thoughts and perceptions. Stress-related Disorders: Resulting from chronic or traumatic stress.
