food and drinks

Pune offers a vibrant food scene, from iconic street foods and local drinks to top restaurants and exciting food festivals for every kind of foodie. Must-Try Street Foods and Drinks Vada Pav (Garden Vada Pav, Camp): A spicy potato fritter in a soft bun with chutneys and fried chilies, a quintessential Pune snack. Dal Khichadi (Hindavi Swarajya, JM Road): Maharashtrian comfort food made with rice and lentils, served with kadhi and chutneys. Sabudana Vada (Shrinathji, Raviwar Peth): Crispy fritters, popular year-round for their perfect spice balance. Tawa Pulao (Sagar Fastfood, Kharadi): Spicy rice dish with vegetables and masalas, perfect with fried green chilies. Burj Khalifa Dosa (Ravi Famous Dosa, Viman Nagar): Towering layered dosa with fillings, a visual and culinary delight. Mastani (Gujar Cold Drinks, Budhwar Peth): Creamy milkshake topped with ice cream and dry fruits, an original Pune specialty. Matki Bhel (Mama Bhel, Sarasbaug): Healthy chaat with sprouted beans, raw mango, and chutneys. Cold Coffee (Richie Rich, BVP): Ice-cream-topped creamy cold coffee, a refreshing student favorite. Chole Kulche (Teri Rajput Dairy, Kothrud): Fluffy kulchas served with spicy chickpea curry.
